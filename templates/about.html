Welcome to the Calorie Prediction Website, your go-to tool for managing and understanding your caloric intake and expenditure...

For Lower Weight

If you are aiming to gain weight or build muscle, consider incorporating these foods into your diet:

  • Avocados
  • Whole grains (e.g., brown rice, quinoa)
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Lean meats (e.g., chicken, turkey)
  • Legumes (e.g., lentils, chickpeas)
  • Healthy oils (e.g., olive oil, coconut oil)
  • Full-fat dairy products (if not lactose intolerant)

For High Weight

If you are looking to manage or reduce weight, these foods can help support your goals:

  • Leafy greens (e.g., spinach, kale)
  • Lean proteins (e.g., fish, tofu)
  • Vegetables (e.g., broccoli, bell peppers)
  • Fruits (e.g., berries, apples)
  • Whole grains (e.g., oats, barley)
  • Legumes (e.g., beans, lentils)
  • Low-fat dairy or dairy alternatives

For Normal Weight

To maintain a balanced diet and overall health, consider these food options:

  • Fruits (e.g., oranges, bananas)
  • Vegetables (e.g., carrots, spinach)
  • Whole grains (e.g., whole wheat bread, brown rice)
  • Lean proteins (e.g., fish, chicken)
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Low-fat dairy products
  • Healthy fats (e.g., avocados, olive oil)
